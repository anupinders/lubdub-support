Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 31, 2025

1. Overview

LubDub is committed to protecting your privacy. This app is designed to work entirely offline and does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No account registration or login is required.
  • No personal information (e.g., name, email, age, location) is collected.
  • No audio recordings are uploaded or shared automatically.
  • No health or biometric data is collected or processed outside the device.
  • No third-party analytics or ads are integrated.

3. What Is Stored

All heart sound recordings are stored locally on your device only. You have full control to manage, delete, or retain these files as per your preference.

4. Optional Security Features

  • LubDub offers optional Face ID or Passcode protection to restrict access to sensitive recordings.
  • This biometric data is handled securely by Apple’s iOS system APIs and never accessed by LubDub itself.

5. Permissions

The app requests access to:

  • Microphone: To capture heart sounds.
  • Face ID (optional): To enhance privacy and security for sensitive recordings.

6. Data Sharing

LubDub does not share your data with any third party. No cloud sync, backup, or remote storage is involved unless you explicitly export and share data yourself.

7. Children’s Privacy

LubDub is intended for general wellness use and is not designed to attract children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal data from minors.

8. Your Rights

You have full control over your data stored within the app. If you uninstall the app, all associated data is permanently removed unless backed up manually by you.

9. Updates to This Policy

This policy may be updated in the future to reflect app changes or compliance needs. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
